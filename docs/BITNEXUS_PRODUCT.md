## The Problem

### Current Market Pain Points

1. **Centralized Control**: Traditional affiliate platforms extract 30-50% of earnings through fees and restrictions
2. **Limited Automation**: Manual work required for content creation, link sharing, and network management
3. **Low Passive Income**: Most affiliate programs require constant active promotion
4. **Trust Issues**: Lack of transparency in earnings, commissions, and platform operations
5. **Complexity**: Multiple platforms needed for different income streams

## Core Features

### 1. Financial Freedom Dashboard

- Real-time earnings tracking
- Income stream breakdown (affiliate, MEV, network)
- Financial freedom progress bar
- Goal setting and tracking
- Quick action checklist
- Daily tips and insights

### 2. Marketplace

- Search/filter by category (Crypto, Health, Tech, Marketing, Automation, Shop, Academy)
- Product tags: Recurring Income, Quick Win, High Commission, Beginner Friendly
- Earning potential calculator
- **Due Diligence Tab**: Research process, product ranking (S–F), security evaluation, audits (CertiK 99.98%, OpenZeppelin, KPMG), ISO 27001 & 27701, transparency ledger
- Product & Vendor Certification Badges
- Affiliate link generator with QR code download (QR Server API, PNG 300x300)
- Product upload for sellers (25 NXC fee)

### 3. Bot Lab (MEV Bot & XAB Bot)

- **MEV Bot Templates**: Arbitrage (10–12% APY), Liquidity (12–15% APY), Flash Loan (15–18% APY)
- **XAB Bot (XRP)**: 10–15% APY
- Staking interface, passive income projection (monthly, yearly, 5-year compound)
- Real-time earnings, active stakes table, earnings history (6 months)
- Withdrawal fee: 0.5%

### 4. Alliance Network

- Tier progression: Bronze → Silver → Gold → Platinum
- Referral codes + QR codes
- Network tree visualization, success stories, global leaderboard
- Live commission ticker
- **Alliance Management**: Members, Settings, Analytics, Recruitment
- **Outreach Tab**: Email, WhatsApp, In-System, Notification; Manual Entry (emails, phone numbers)
- Alliance chat, private forum
- Network statistics

### 5. AI Content Generator

- Product-based content generation
- Marketing assets integration
- Multi-platform: YouTube, Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, X, LinkedIn, Snapchat
- Google Gemini API
- Custom content upload (images, videos, text)
- Content preview, editing, one-click sharing
- NXC credits: 10 credits per generation

### 6. Academy

- Getting Started Course (onboarding, 10-step flow, video, downloadable PDFs)
- Learning Paths: $1K/month (30 days), $5K/month (90 days), Financial Freedom (6 months)
- Live Zoom training (AI Learning, Financial Freedom, Affiliate Marketing)
- Course library (Beginner–Mastery)
- Progress tracking

### 7. Goals & Tracking

- Income, network, product goals
- Visual progress, days remaining
- Achievement celebrations
