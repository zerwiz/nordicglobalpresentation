## Our Approach: The "Hidden Talent" Model

The tech industry faces a talent shortage while brilliant minds are overlooked. Nordic Global Solutions bridges the gap.

### The Broken System — How Traditional Hiring Excludes Talent

| Barrier | Impact |
|---------|--------|
| **Resume Keyword Filter** | ATS systems reject 75% of qualified candidates who don't use exact keyword matches → **−75% talent pool** |
| **"Culture Fit" Interviews** | Subjective bias filters out neurodivergent candidates despite technical excellence → **−60% neurodiverse talent** |
| **Whiteboard Coding** | High-pressure performance tests favor extroverts, miss brilliant introverted thinkers → **−50% deep thinkers** |
| **Degree Requirements** | Excludes self-taught developers with years of real-world experience → **−40% experienced devs** |

**Net Result: 89% Talent Loss** — while companies complain about talent shortage.

### The Nordic Solution — Skills-First Hiring

| Element | Outcome |
|---------|---------|
| **Cognitive Assessment** | Testing logic, pattern recognition & problem-solving—not pedigree → **+300% candidate pool** |
| **Neurodiversity Focus** | Leveraging hyper-focus, pattern recognition & systematic thinking → **+30% bug detection rate** |
| **Structured Mentorship** | Senior Tech Leads provide clear guidance & support → **95% retention rate** |
| **Sensory-Friendly Workplaces** | Quiet zones, flexible schedules, async communication |
| **Real-World Projects** | Evaluate skills through actual work, not interviews |

**Net Result: Hidden Talent Unlocked** — building world-class teams from overlooked genius.
